0101	Normal	ABSTRACT
0102	Normal	Nowadays, the application of information technology in healthcare is increasingly important to improve management efficiency and service quality. In dental clinics, managing patients, appointments, treatment records, and dental services plays a significant role in daily operations. However, many clinics still face difficulties in managing data and tracking information effectively.
0103	Normal	The project entitled “Smart Dental Clinic Management System” aims to develop a web-based management system that supports the management of patients, dentists, dental services, appointments, and treatment records. The system allows customers to register accounts, book appointments online, view their treatment history, and evaluate service quality. At the same time, it assists dentists and administrators in managing patient information, work schedules, medical records, and clinic statistics.
0104	Normal	In addition to basic management functions, the system integrates several smart features such as a dental consultation chatbot, automatic appointment reminders, and customer feedback analysis to enhance user experience and improve management efficiency.
0105	Normal	Keywords: Dental clinic, clinic management, appointment booking, web application, chatbot.
