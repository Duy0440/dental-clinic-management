# Study Notes Full

## Bai 1

### Cau hoi: Bai toan thuc te cua de tai la gi?

De tai xuat phat tu nhu cau so hoa quy trinh tham kham va quan ly benh nhan tai phong kham nha khoa hien dai. He thong huong toi lien ket cac khau dat lich, tiep nhan, tham kham, ho so dieu tri, hoa don va cham soc khach hang tren cung mot nen tang, thay vi quan ly roi rac bang so tay hoac file rieng le.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao de tai nay khong phai web CRUD binh thuong?

De tai nay khong chi la mot website them sua xoa du lieu, ma la mot he thong quan ly hoan chinh theo quy trinh nghiep vu cua phong kham nha khoa. Cac phan he nhu benh nhan, lich hen, ho so dieu tri, hoa don, dashboard va AI chatbot co lien ket voi nhau, tao thanh mot luong xu ly thuc te.

### Cau hoi: AI trong he thong dung de lam gi?

AI trong he thong dong vai tro ho tro truyen thong, tu van co ban va bo sung kien thuc nha khoa cho khach hang. Trong boi canh co nhieu thong tin sai lech hoac tu van khong minh bach, chatbot AI giup nguoi dung hieu ro hon ve quy trinh tham kham, dich vu co ban va cac luu y truoc khi den phong kham. AI khong thay the bac si, ma chi dong vai tro ho tro dinh huong.

### Cau hoi: Khach hang co nen co chuc nang xem lich hen sap toi va xem lai ket qua kham khong?

Co, nen co. Xem lich hen sap toi giup khach khong quen lich va tang tinh tien loi. Xem lai ket qua kham hoac lich su dieu tri giup khach theo doi tinh trang rang mieng, nho duoc da kham gi, da dieu tri gi va khi nao can tai kham.

### Cau hoi: Ung dung nay co phu hop thuc te khong?

Co, neu gioi han pham vi hop ly. He thong phu hop voi phong kham nha khoa quy mo nho den vua, vi dap ung duoc cac nghiep vu cot loi nhu quan ly benh nhan, lich hen, ho so dieu tri, hoa don, thong ke va chatbot tu van co ban.

### Cau hoi: Tai sao phai tach 3 vai tro admin, dentist, customer?

Vi moi nhom nguoi dung co nhu cau khac nhau. Admin quan ly he thong, nha si xu ly chuyen mon, con khach hang su dung cac chuc nang dich vu. Tach vai tro giup phan quyen ro rang va sat voi thuc te.

## Bai 2

### Cau hoi: Vi sao can bang users rieng thay vi luu het vao patients hoac dentists?

Vi users la bang xac thuc chung cho toan he thong, luu thong tin dang nhap nhu username, mat khau va vai tro. Con patients va dentists la doi tuong nghiep vu, co them thong tin rieng phuc vu kham chua benh. Tach nhu vay giup de phan quyen, de quan ly dang nhap va de mo rong he thong.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao appointments la bang trung tam?

Vi appointments la diem ket noi giua benh nhan, nha si, dich vu va thoi gian kham. Day la diem bat dau cua luong nghiep vu tu dat lich den tham kham, cap nhat ho so dieu tri va lap hoa don, nen no la bang trung tam cua he thong.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao nen co invoice_details?

Vi mot hoa don trong thuc te co the gom nhieu dich vu khac nhau, khong chi mot dong tong tien. Bang invoice_details giup luu chi tiet tung dich vu, so luong, don gia va thanh tien, dung hon voi nghiep vu va de mo rong sau nay.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao chatbot_logs lam de tai noi bat hon?

Vi chatbot_logs cho thay module AI khong chi la giao dien minh hoa ma co du lieu su dung thuc te. Bang nay giup luu lai cau hoi va cau tra loi, tu do co the thong ke nhu cau nguoi dung va cai thien chatbot trong tuong lai.

### Cau hoi: Neu le tan hay admin cap nhat ho so dieu tri thi co hop ly khong?

Hop ly nhat la tach giua phan hanh chinh va phan chuyen mon. Admin hoac le tan co the nhap thong tin, scan giay to, tai tep dinh kem va quan ly ho so. Nha si van nen la nguoi chiu trach nhiem ve noi dung chuyen mon nhu chan doan, ket qua kham va huong dieu tri.

## Bai 3

### Cau hoi: Tai sao chon username thay vi email de dang nhap?

Vi doi tuong nguoi dung cua he thong co ca benh nhan lon tuoi, nhieu nguoi khong quen dung email hoac khong nho email. Chon username lam thong tin dang nhap chinh giup he thong than thien hon voi nguoi dung thuc te cua phong kham.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao email chi nen de tuy chon?

Vi khong phai benh nhan nao cung co email hoac can dung email de dang nhap. De email o dang tuy chon giup he thong linh hoat hon, nhung van co cho de luu neu can thong bao hoac mo rong sau nay.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao attachment_url trong medical_records la hop ly?

Vi trong thuc te phong kham co the can luu file scan, hinh anh hoac giay to lien quan den ho so benh an. Truong nay giup he thong gan voi thuc te hon ma van giu duoc muc do don gian de code.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao unit_price phai luu trong invoice_details?

Vi gia dich vu co the thay doi theo thoi gian. Khi lap hoa don, can luu don gia tai thoi diem do de du lieu lich su khong bi sai neu gia trong bang services thay doi sau nay.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao issued_by trong invoices la huu ich?

Vi no cho biet ai la nguoi lap hoa don, thuong la admin hoac le tan. Truong nay giup kiem tra quy trinh, doi soat nghiep vu va tang tinh thuc te cho he thong.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao chua can tach bang file dinh kem rieng?

Vi voi pham vi do an hien tai, mot truong attachment_url trong medical_records la du de mo phong viec luu file scan hoac tai lieu. Tach bang rieng dung hon o he thong lon, nhung se lam do an phuc tap qua som.

## Bai 4

### Cau hoi: Tai sao phai tao database rieng cho do an?

Vi em muon tach rieng du lieu do an voi cac bai thuc hanh khac de de quan ly, de trien khai va tranh nham lan trong qua trinh phat trien.

### Cau hoi: Tai sao dung PostgreSQL?

Vi nguoi thuc hien da co san pgAdmin 4 va quen voi PostgreSQL, nen viec chon PostgreSQL giup trien khai on dinh hon, giam mat thoi gian cai dat va xu ly cong cu. PostgreSQL van rat phu hop voi he thong du lieu quan he nhu phong kham nha khoa.

### Cau hoi: Tai sao trong PostgreSQL dung SERIAL thay vi AUTO_INCREMENT?

Vi em dung PostgreSQL nen cot khoa chinh tu tang duoc khai bao bang SERIAL. Neu dung MySQL thi thuong se dung AUTO_INCREMENT.

### Cau hoi: Tai sao users la bang nen dau tien?

Vi users la bang xac thuc chung cho toan he thong. Cac bang nghiep vu nhu patients va dentists deu co the lien ket toi users, nen can tao users truoc.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao patients.user_id co the NULL?

Vi trong thuc te phong kham co the tao ho so benh nhan truoc, con tai khoan dang nhap co the tao sau. De user_id cho phep NULL giup he thong linh hoat hon voi nghiep vu thuc te.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao dentists.user_id phai NOT NULL?

Vi nha si la nguoi dung noi bo cua he thong, bat buoc phai co tai khoan de dang nhap va lam viec. Do do user_id cua dentists nen bat buoc ton tai.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao price nen dung DECIMAL?

Vi du lieu tien te can do chinh xac cao. DECIMAL phu hop hon FLOAT khi luu tru gia dich vu va so tien hoa don.

### Cau hoi: Vi sao username nen de UNIQUE?

Vi ten dang nhap phai la duy nhat de he thong phan biet tung tai khoan va tranh xung dot khi dang nhap.

### Cau hoi: Services la bang doc lap nghia la gi?

Nghia la bang services khong phu thuoc vao bang users, patients hay dentists de duoc tao ra. Day la bang danh muc, luu cac dich vu co san cua phong kham va cac bang khac se tham chieu den no sau.

### Cau hoi: Hom nay da lam duoc gi o Bai 4?

Hom nay em tao database rieng cho do an tren PostgreSQL va xay dung 4 bang nen gom users, patients, dentists va services. Day la cac bang goc de lam co so cho cac bang nghiep vu nhu lich hen, ho so dieu tri va hoa don o buoc sau.
